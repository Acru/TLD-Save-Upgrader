The Long Dark Save Converter

This tool will convert your old saves for v.???.

Save location

PC: C:\Users\USERNAME\AppData\Local\Hinterland\TheLongDark
Mac: /users/USERNAME/.local/share/Hinterland/TheLongDark
Linux: /home/USERNAME/.local/share/Hinterland/TheLongDark

Instructions

  • Select your save files ("global", "boot" and "scenes.zip")
  • Click convert
  • Place the downloaded save in the "TheLongDark" folder

You can not change the save slot by just changing the file name. If you need your save in different slot, convert it again.
